        Steps
        Gather Sources
        Determine what your sources have. Some business rules are imposed on you from laws and regulations, other might be company standards. Other business rules express the goals of what the business modeling effort is to achieve. 
        Express the Rules
        Express the business rules using a rigorous and formal language, consider the audience to make sure they can be effectively communicated. For more on categories and styles for business rules, see Guidelines: Business Rules . 
        Evaluate Your Results
        Check your results to verify that you have followed a consistent style when defining the rules and that they are not inconsistent in what they state.
